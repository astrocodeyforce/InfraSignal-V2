Security

Content Moderation & Safety

Every photo uploaded to InfraSignal is automatically analyzed by our content moderation system before publication. This system screens for prohibited content including violence, nudity, weapons, drugs, offensive material, and other inappropriate imagery. Photos that violate our content policies are automatically rejected. This processing occurs in real time to ensure harmful content is never published on the platform.

Application Security

  • Input Validation: All user inputs are validated and sanitized to prevent common web application attacks.
  • Access Controls: Administrative functions are restricted to authorized staff accounts with role-based permissions.
  • Email Security: Outbound emails are sent using TLS encryption, ensuring messages are delivered securely.

Responsible Disclosure

We value the work of independent security researchers. If you discover a security vulnerability in the InfraSignal platform, we ask that you:

  1. Report the vulnerability privately to [% c.cobrand.contact_email %] with the subject line “Security Vulnerability Report”
  2. Provide sufficient detail to reproduce the issue, including steps, URLs, and screenshots where applicable
  3. Allow us a reasonable timeframe (at least 90 days) to investigate and address the issue before any public disclosure
  4. Do not access, modify, or delete data belonging to other users during your research

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours and keep you informed of our progress toward a fix.

Incident Response

In the event of a security incident that compromises personal data, we will:

  • Investigate and contain the incident promptly
  • Notify affected users as required by applicable state data breach notification laws
  • Report the incident to relevant regulatory authorities where required
  • Take corrective measures to prevent recurrence
